Hello!

I make games built upon innovative concepts, strong core mechanics, and minimalist design.

I've gained experience in game design by bringing my game Hexopolis to market for global distribution, as well as being a researcher on a CHI publication and prototyping games. Through simple mechanics which beget complex consequences, I aim to make games that investigate the systems surrounding us in the real world as well as the mechanics that dictate life.

Some of the games that have shaped me as a designer include The Witness, Prey (2017), and The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild.

I graduated from Northeastern University in 2024 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Game Development.
