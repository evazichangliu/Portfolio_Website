Journalist

Zichang is a passionate bilingual journalist based in Boston, Massachusetts.

She graduated from Boston University with an M.S. in Journalism in May 2025. Now working at Worldcrunch, an international news agency based in Pairs, she brings her unique global perspectives into her productions for audiences all around the world.

She also specializes in investigative data-driven reporting and multimedia storytelling. She has covered breaking news in Politics, Healthcare, Environment, Science, and Social justice issues. She is keen for finding underreported stories and have the truth to be told with hidden voices, and her multimedia storytelling techniques from anywhere the story is happening.

Her reporting has been published through Worldcrunch, the Associated Press, The Independent, ABC News, the Daily Hampshire Gazette, the Worcester Telegram, the Greenfield Recorder, and Boston University News Service.
